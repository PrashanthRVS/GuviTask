Kazi Nazrul Islam

The national poet of Bangladesh


Rebel Poet

Kazi Narul Islam is the national poet of Banglades who is especially recongnized as a Bidrohi Kobi(rebel poet) around the country. Nazrul is a famous poet, muciscian and philosopher of Banglades. The famous poet Nazrul's writings deal with the revolution of that period of the British Government. His revolutionary attempt helped him to be a national poet of Bangladesh. He has raised his voice against any kinds of fascism and oppression and go poupularity swiftly around the Indian Subcontinent.

Awsome Writer

Narul's writings explored themes such as love, freedom, humanity, and revolution. He opposed all forms of bigotry and fundamentalism, inclusding religious, caste-based and gender-based. Through his career, Nazrul worte short stories, novel, and essays but is best know for his songs and poem, He pioneered new music forms such as Bengali gahzals. Nazrul wrote and composed music for nearly 4000, songs(many recorded on HMV and gramophone records), collectively know as Nazrul Geeti.
